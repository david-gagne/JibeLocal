Frequently Asked Questions

How can a book a table for rent?

You can book a massage table for rent by sending

1. Your name,
2. Phone number, and
3. The date and time you'd like to rent

To Hello@JibeLocal.com

OR

Calling / Texting us at (786) 496-3198

What cities/regions do you serve?

We currently only rent massage tables in Broward County, Florida.

How many days can I rent a massage table?

Massage tables can be rented for a maximum of five (5) days, if available.

Who's table am I renting?

The massage tables are rented by local massage therapists from Fort Lauderdale to Pembroke Pines and other areas of Broward County.

How much does it cost to rent a massage table?

Massage tables are rented at a rate of $20 per day and is to be paid upon confirmation of booking.

If renting for multiple days,

Rental Price = ($20 x # of Days Requested)

Do you rent massage chairs?

Yes, we also offer massage therapy chairs for rent at the same rate as massage tables.

I'd like to rent out my massage table, how can I get started?

Great question! To get started renting out your massage table with JibeLocal, please send

1. Your name, and
2. Phone number

To Hello@JibeLocal.com

OR

Call / Text it to us at (786) 496-3198
